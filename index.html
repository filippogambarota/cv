PhD Student - University of Padova

I’m a PhD student in Psychological Sciences at the University of Padua, supervised by Prof. Paola Sessa from the CoLab Research Group. My research interests regard social and affective neuroscience and visual consciousness. I am also interested in unconscious information processing and visual working memory. In parallel, I’m interested in the topic of trasparent and reproducible research in particular using reproducibile tools for my academic life.
